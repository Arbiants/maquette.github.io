Arbiants is a collection of 10,000 unique, programmatically generated, fully ON-CHAIN Layer2 NFTs !



What does that means ?


- Each Arbiant is absolutly unique ! With more than 900,000 possible combinations, the collection represents only 1,11% of all possibilities !
